About Us

Rooted Web grew from one specialty: building websites that work. Based in Cape Girardeau, we cultivate brand growth through effective marketing and exceptional websites. We help businesses grow by assisting with everything—from redefining a web presence to elevating a brand’s national reach through website design and development, digital marketing and advertising, custom content creation, and software integrations.

The Project

Develop a marketing dashboard solution for our clients to quickly and easily view project milestones and creative proofs, plus review snapshots of key analytics, metrics and budget.

Dashboard should have a time frame/comparison feature for 7 days, 28 days, month, month-to-date, 90 days and custom. Analytics should pull from GoogleAds, Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Facebook, Facebook Ads, LinkedIn, LinkedIn Ads, Instagram, Twitter and Twitter Ads.

Your Responsibilities

  • Work with a team of 3-4 other junior developers to build dashboard solution
  • Code in Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS and JavaScript
  • Code, deploy and test software within expected time and scope

The Interview

We are a young and close-knit team so finding someone who fits is a top priority.

  • Do you work well with others?
  • Do you enjoy learning and push yourself beyond the expected?
  • How do you react when presented with a difficult problem?
  • How do you respond to critiques or criticism from clients and teammates?
