About Us

I am an entrepreneur with a passion to putting solutions to problems. I have recently been a co-owner of a local business called TNT Health & Fitness. Through my experience of being a business owner I’ve experienced the amount of time, energy, and resources wasted with running a small business - everything from putting numbers together (cashflow, expenses, client payments, etc.), to growing your staff, organizing policies & procedures, hiring the right staff, marketing, sales, taxes, etc. You get the picture. When running a business, very little time is actually spent on the craft that you are the best at. I want to empower businesses and other entrepreneurs of what they can really offer to the world without the side of business scaring them away.

The Project

For a local business, we are needing to create a web app to help them with scheduling for their staff so each employee knows their schedule without having to go to the shop to find out. The solution will need employee communication that gives the employees and owner to communicate with each other so everyone knows what is going on at the shop. We’ll also need a task management system so that the owner of the business can develop tasks for needs to be done before the day starts. This can need to be put on the schedule for what days tasks need to be done and assign who is responsible for each task.

Your Responsibilities

  • Working with a team of 1-2 other junior developers to accomplish the goals stated above
  • Code in Ruby
  • Code, test, and deploy
  • Effectively communicate
