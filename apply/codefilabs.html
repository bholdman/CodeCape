CodefiLabs

About Us

Codefi opened its doors in the historic Federal Building in November 2014 as the area’s first co-working space and technology incubator with a larger vision for economic growth to be a catalyst to increase the formation and growth of technology-based companies in our region. We moved to the historic Marquette Tower in Fall 2016 as the anchor of the Marquette Tech District.

So, we lead by example. While we do develop custom software for mid-market companies, we also invest in and build our own concepts and are the technical founders for venture-backed startups, like carGO Technologies.

The Project

If you choose to work with Codefi, you’ll be helping us build out systems to automate the Youth Coding League. The first will be a check-in system for the semester-ending All Star party. If we have enough time, we’ll work on building a onboarding process for schools, coaches, and students.

Your Responsibilities

  • Work with a team of 3-4 other junior developers to build solution
  • Code in Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Code, deploy, and test software within expected time and scope

The Interview

At Codefi, we typically do not advertise job openings. We generally know who we want to hire and reach out to them to meet with us. What we look for in an individual:

  • Ability to work in a highly-unstructured environment, built by design
  • Proof of being a self-starter, or getting shit done
  • If not much experience, then a sensational curiosity and interest in learning
  • Culture fit. Are you a dick? Probably won’t fit in here. Are you lazy? No thanks, we don’t just work 9-5. We want highly-motivated individuals who care about outcomes over hours and get shit done. But that also means we don’t track what time you come in or when you leave and there are no limits on vacation time. We believe in giving, not taking. We believe in helping others before helping yourself.
