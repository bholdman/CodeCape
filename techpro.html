Healthcare Hackathon

July 19-21, 2019

MTi—in partnership with Saint Francis Healthcare System—will host a healthcare hackathon awarding $50,000 in cash and prizes. During the hackathon, participating teams of all levels will work collaboratively with industry experts to build tools, apps, and platforms to improve healthcare experiences. Technologists, students, and creatives from around the nation are invited to participate. The healthcare hackathon will be held July 19-21, 2019, in Cape Girardeau, Missouri.
