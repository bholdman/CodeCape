Youth Education

Introductory and intermediate programs for youth and young adults that teach basic programming and problem solving skills that will benefit them no matter what career they choose.

Upcoming Programs

Code Camp: Coding with The Finch

July 10-14, 2017

3-6 grade kids will work to connect the dots from playing online to creating online to real world jobs. Students will learn how to think computationally and solve problems as they make their Finch, a small robot designed for young students with simple drag and drop coding, do sweet dance moves and navigate through obstacle courses. It will be a super fun week as students work in teams, experiment and see just how code translates into the physical world. $75 fee.
