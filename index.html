What We Do

About CodeCape

CodeCape is a regional initiative by Codefi to provide informal education, competitions and social activities to link aspiring youth and adult coders with local experts in southeast Missouri.

Advisory Council

The CodeCape advisory council is made up of local tech professionals that help to guide the direction of programs offered by CodeCape to ensure we are providing the skills needed to be successful.
